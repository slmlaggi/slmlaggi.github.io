Dev Diary

Date: 18-07-24

The 12th of July marked the end of term, and I dove right into my summer TODO list. Naturally, I kicked things off with the profile page. I initially estimated about 15 hours to transform my rough ideas into a polished, device-friendly masterpiece (Spoiler alert: mobile web UI/UX design is not my favorite!). Of course, it took much longer than planned, but I still had learnt a lot from this experience. Since I didn’t track every single change during the development process, I’ll share a simple timeline of my thoughts and progress in this entry.

Day 0 [12-07-2024]: The about page.

I started with an early draft of the About page. Ignoring mobile resizing, I created a full-screen section with about ten words per page — definitely an odd design choice! I also added favicon icons to spruce things up. After around four hours of work, I had laid out my initial ideas for the page.

Cumulative hours count: 4

Day 1 [13-07-2024]: Socials hell

I spent the entire weekend (13th and 14th July) working without committing any changes because I wanted to finish the Socials part before going live. Day 1 was a bit chaotic; I wasn’t at my desk all day but still managed around six hours trying to make the Socials page smooth. I borrowed some widget ideas from bento.me, which I had used as my socials link page beforehand. Although it worked, it felt a bit clunky to deploy on a single page.

Cumulative hours count: 10

Day 2 [14-07-2024]: More dev work

I decided it was better to create a separate page for my playlists instead of cramming them into the already cluttered Socials page. More debugging ensued, along with some reworking of JavaScript functions and styles. I also stayed up until 5:30 AM to watch Spain win the Euros.

Cumulative hours count: 17

Day 3 [15-07-2024]: Mobile go brr

I spent an hour or two redesigning the mobile layout to improve user-friendliness. Productivity was low since I was pretty sleepy after the previous night.

Cumulative hours count: 20

Day 4 [16-07-2024]: Fixed color palette and some more positioning stuff

This day was a mix of tidying up directories and making backups, along with bursts of inspiration for color choices and positioning tweaks. The site was mostly complete at this point, though the About page still needed some fixing.

Cumulative hours count: 24

Day 5 [17-07-2024]: Brainf**k

I woke up determined to finish everything today. I revamped the About page functionalities and redesign in about three to four hours. Creating the skills tab was interesting but came with its challenges. Just as I was ready to publish, I remembered my past experience with TypeScript and decided to rewrite the entire index.js in TypeScript to refresh my skills. This led to seven hours of rewriting and troubleshooting. By nearly 11 PM, everything was finally working despite having a few minor design issues.

Cumulative hours count: 38

Day 6 [18-07-2024] (TODAY): Bug hunting and fixing

There were about 10 or so minor bugs and places there could be improved, however nothing really changed in the design and alignment aside from small fixes. Cleaning up the entire project directory and stuff later its finally done! Only things left is to add more blog entries, hopefully at least once per month or more frequently.

Cumulative hours count: 45
